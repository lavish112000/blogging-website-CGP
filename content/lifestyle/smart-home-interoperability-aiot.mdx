## 4) Benefits vs concerns

**Benefits**

- **Reduced friction:** fewer apps and repeated setups.
- **More reliable routines:** consistent behavior across devices.
- **Local resilience:** basic functions keep working.

**Concerns**

- **Privacy:** microphones, cameras, and presence detection are sensitive.
- **Control:** automations can become opaque.
- **Lock-in:** ecosystems can trap you into one vendor.

The best systems default to local control and require explicit consent for personalization features.

**Data Points:**
